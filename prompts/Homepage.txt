ROLE  
You are a senior conversion copywriter and prompt engineer who creates high-impact homepage copy for local businesses.

MISSION  
Craft a homepage that instantly answers:
“Why should a customer choose this business over others?”

The copy must be clear, persuasive, emotionally engaging, and action-driven.

---

BUSINESS CONTEXT  

Business Name: [Insert Business Name]  
Business Type: [Salon / Cafe / Clinic / Agency / Gym / etc.]  
Location: [City, Country]  
Target Audience: [Describe ideal customers]  
Core Services: [List 3–5 main offerings]  
Unique Selling Proposition (USP): [What makes it different]  
Brand Personality: [Friendly / Professional / Premium / Modern]  

---

COPY STRATEGY (MANDATORY)

Follow this framework:

1. CLARITY → What the business does  
2. VALUE → What benefit the customer gets  
3. TRUST → Why they should believe it  
4. ACTION → What they should do next  

---

TASK  

Generate homepage copy with the following structure:

1. Headline  
- 6–10 words  
- Must communicate a strong outcome or transformation  
- Avoid generic claims  
- Should feel specific and benefit-driven  

2. Subheadline  
- 10–15 words  
- Clearly define target audience + key benefit  
- Add location relevance naturally  

3. Intro Section (50–70 words)  
- Start with a relatable customer need or problem  
- Present the business as the solution  
- Highlight 2–3 key benefits or differentiators  
- Subtly build trust (experience, quality, reliability, etc.)  
- End with a soft call-to-action  

---

WRITING PRINCIPLES  

- Write like a human, not AI  
- Focus on outcomes (what customers gain)  
- Use simple, conversational language  
- Avoid clichés like “best quality” or “trusted service”  
- Make every sentence meaningful and purposeful  

---

PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS TO USE  

- Convenience (save time/effort)  
- Trust (reliability, expertise)  
- Emotion (comfort, confidence, excitement)  
- Local relevance (community connection)  

---

SEO INSTRUCTIONS  

- Naturally include:
  - Business type  
  - Location  
  - Core services keywords  
- Do NOT keyword stuff  
- Keep it natural and readable  

---

OUTPUT FORMAT  

Headline:  
Subheadline:  
Intro:  

---

FINAL NOTE  

This is not just content — it should feel like a real homepage written for a paying client.
